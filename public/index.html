What is Alebrije?

Alebrije is a vibrant meme coin inspired by the colorful Mexican folk art sculptures of fantastical creatures. Just as these art pieces bring joy and wonder, our token aims to bring vibrancy and creativity to the Solana ecosystem.

Tokenomics

Token Distribution

  • 40% Community & Airdrops
  • 20% Liquidity Pool
  • 15% Ecosystem Development
  • 10% Marketing & Partnerships
  • 10% Treasury Reserve
  • 5% Founders & Advisors

Transaction Tax

Every transaction incurs a 5% tax, distributed as follows:

  • 2% Burn - Permanently removed from circulation
  • 1% Reflections - Redistributed to holders
  • 1% Marketing - Funds promotional activities
  • 1% Treasury - Supports community development
